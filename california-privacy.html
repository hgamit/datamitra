Supplemental Notice for California Residents

If you are a resident of California, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. This notice supplements our Privacy Notice with information required by the CCPA.

Personal Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identifiers (name, email, phone, postal address)
  • Commercial information (transaction history, engagement records)
  • Internet or electronic network activity (browsing history, cookies)
  • Professional information (job title, company, role)
  • Inferences drawn from personal information

Your California Privacy Rights

Under the CCPA, California residents have the right to:

1. Right to Know

You have the right to request what personal information we have collected about you, including the sources of that information, the purposes for collection, and the recipients with whom we share it.

2. Right to Delete

You have the right to request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (such as when we need it to fulfill your request or comply with legal obligations).

3. Right to Correct

You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate personal information.

4. Right to Opt-Out

You have the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. While we do not sell personal information in the traditional sense, we may share information with service providers and partners in ways that could be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under California law.

5. Right to Non-Discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights. We will not deny you goods or services, charge different prices, or provide different quality of service based on your exercise of these rights.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a request to:

We will verify your identity and respond to your request within 45 days. If you submit a request through an authorized agent, we may require proof of authorization.

California Consumer Privacy Notice at Collection

At or before collection, we provide consumers with a notice that includes:

  • The categories of personal information to be collected
  • The business or commercial purpose for collection
  • Whether the personal information will be sold or shared
  • How to exercise consumer rights under the CCPA
