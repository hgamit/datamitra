Modern Slavery Statement

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Our Commitment

Datamitra, Inc. is committed to maintaining the highest ethical standards in our business operations and supply chains. We are committed to ensuring that there is no slavery or human trafficking in our supply chains or business.

Our Organization

Datamitra is a boutique data and AI consulting firm based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. We provide advisory services to organizations looking to unlock measurable business outcomes from data and artificial intelligence initiatives.

Supply Chain and Subcontractors

Our supply chain consists primarily of service providers in software, telecommunications, and professional services. We carefully select partners who share our values and commitment to ethical business practices. We contractually require all of our suppliers and subcontractors to comply with all applicable laws regarding labor practices, wages, and working conditions.

Policies and Due Diligence

We maintain policies requiring:

  • Compliance with all applicable labor and employment laws
  • Fair wages and reasonable working hours
  • Safe and healthy working conditions
  • Prohibition of forced labor and child labor
  • Freedom of association and collective bargaining

Training and Reporting

Our team members receive training on labor practices and ethical business conduct. We encourage our employees to report any suspected violations through appropriate channels, and we maintain mechanisms for investigating and addressing concerns.

Ongoing Commitment

We remain committed to assessing and improving our practices to ensure we maintain the highest standards of ethical conduct and eliminate any risk of slavery or human trafficking in our operations and supply chains.
